Jurassic Park Map

Jurassic Park is located on an island called Isla Nublar. Jurassic Park has locations for its staff, visitors, and dinosaurs. Many visitors will first be at the dock at the east and travel to the visitor center. Going on their tour, visitors will occasionally come across our concession stands. Our concession stands provide many useful things such as foods, sweets, snacks, and more. Please note that, if you want to buy any product in Jurassic Park, you have to purchase the JurassicPAY currency specially for our establishment. We will not accept all currency from all countries.
